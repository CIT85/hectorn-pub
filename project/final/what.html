Key Points About Light Pollution

On our main page, we listed the following points about light pollution, but here is an deeper look at what we are discussing when it comes to the effects

Glare:

Intense brightness from poorly shielded lights that can be uncomfortable or even hazardous, like when driving at night and encountering bright lights from oncoming vehicles.

SkyGlow:

The brightening of the night sky over inhabited areas due to the scattering of artificial light by particles and gases in the atmosphere, which reduces visibility of stars and celestial objects.

Light Trespass:

Unwanted or excessive artificial light that spills over into areas where it is not needed or wanted, such as into residential homes from nearby streetlights or commercial buildings.

Clutter:

Overabundance of light fixtures or signs in urban areas, leading to a visually chaotic environment and wasted energy.

Disruption of ecosystems:

Artificial light at night can disrupt the behaviors and habitats of wildlife, affecting their natural rhythms, migration patterns, and reproduction cycles.
